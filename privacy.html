Privacy Policy
How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.
📅 Last updated: April 2025

Group Ride Coffee LLC respects your privacy. We only collect what we need to serve you and never sell your personal information to third parties.
01
What We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you place an order, subscribe to our coffee drops, register for rides, or contact us.

  • Name and contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe — we never store card details)
  • Order history and subscription preferences
  • Event participation records
  • Communications you send us

We also automatically collect basic usage data when you visit our website, including your browser type, pages visited, and general location.

02
How We Use It

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders and subscriptions
  • Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and receipts
  • Notify you about upcoming coffee drops, rides, and events
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Improve our products, services, and website
We will only send you marketing communications if you opt in. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying STOP or contacting us directly.
03
Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We only share your data with trusted third parties necessary to operate our business:

  • Stripe — payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Shipping carriers — for order delivery
  • Email service providers — for order and event communications

All third-party partners are contractually required to protect your information and use it only for the specified purpose.

04
Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and remember your preferences. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

05
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at (224) 490-6981 or visit us at 10548 S Western Ave Ste 2R, Chicago, IL 60643.
